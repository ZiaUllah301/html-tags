Imran Khan

About Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a prominent Pakistani politician, former cricketer, and philanthropist. He gained fame as a cricket player and led the Pakistan cricket team to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup.

Early Life

Imran Khan was born on October 5, 1952, in Lahore, Pakistan. He studied at the University of Oxford and later joined the cricket world.

Political Career

Imran Khan founded the political party Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) in 1996. He became the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan in August 2018.

Philanthropy

Aside from his political and cricketing endeavors, Imran Khan is also known for his philanthropic work. He has established the Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital & Research Centre and Namal University.
